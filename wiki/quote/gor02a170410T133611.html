8 Attachment principles

Schlagwort: [Attachment principle] [Friction] [Glue] [Expansion anchor] [Sucker] [Spacer] [Clamp] [Snap] [Lock] [﻿Hook]

Typ: [Klassifikation] [Aufzählung]

Zitat:

There are eight fundamental classes of attachment principles: (1) hooks, (2) lock or snap, (3) clamp, (4) spacer, (5) sucker, (6) expansion anchor, (7) glue, and (8) friction, which can occur in various combinations.
